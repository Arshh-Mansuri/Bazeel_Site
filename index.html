Tableau Designing and Fabrication

APEE GRAPHICS's portfolio encompasses a wide variety of Tableau's designing and fabrication from the late 1984 onwards. These range from Nation level and State level. Four times award winner at National level and twelve times at State level.

Sculpture and Illustration work

APEE GRAPHICS's portfolio involves creating 2d and 3d works of art, using materials like clay, fibre, metal, wood etc. Apee Graphics developed Gujarat Vidyapith Tribal Museum after detail study of vibrant tribal life and culture. Museum showcases different kinds of homes, occupation, crafts, jewelry, tools etc with 3d work of art.
