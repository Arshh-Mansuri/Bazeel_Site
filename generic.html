About Us

Brief History

APEE Graphics, founded by the visionary Mr. Aakir Jaakir Panj in 1984, has established itself as a versatile and innovative design firm with a distinguished reputation. Under Mr. Panj’s creative leadership, the company has contributed to numerous prestigious projects, including the Republic Day Parade in Delhi, where his tableau designs were prominently featured. He has also played a pivotal role in various Gujarat government initiatives and GUJCOST (Gujarat Council on Science & Technology) projects, blending artistry with public service. APEE Graphics has earned significant recognition, winning four national-level awards and twelve state-level accolades. Specializing in tableau design and fabrication, their expertise extends to a wide range of creative services, including sculpture, illustration work, stall design, and architectural and interior design. The firm’s contributions to cultural preservation are highlighted by projects like the Gujarat Vidyapith Tribal Museum, which showcases vibrant tribal life through detailed 3D artworks. APEE Graphics is also committed to sustainability, working alongside young architect Bazeel Panj to create functional, environmentally conscious spaces. With a diverse portfolio and a passion for artistic excellence, APEE Graphics continues to leave a lasting impact on the design industry.
